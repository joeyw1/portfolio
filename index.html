Project 3 : Data Storytelling Project - Credit Card Customer Churn

Tool used: Tableau

The project's goal is to identify potential credit card churners, contact them before they cancel their accounts, and present the findings to key management during the meeting.

To make it easier for key management to remember the information, I used the Freytag pyramid narrative model to tell a story about the data. It outlined the following concepts: introduction (exposition), rising action, climax, falling action, and resolution. After the setting of the scene, the audience is led to the main insight (climax), which is followed by a recommendation or resolution.
